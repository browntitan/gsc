Ollama Supply Chain Pipeline Sample Queries

Notes
- Clause 10 is the best clean section-aware test file for Intent, Common Exceptions / Suggested Responses, and Suggested Responses.
- Clause 12 is the best broader retrieval test file for warranty-related questions.
- Supported termset formats include:
  - termset 3
  - termet 3
  - T&C 3
  - tc 003
  - CTM-P-ST-003

Single-Turn Queries

General retrieval
- What does clause 12 say about warranty period for termset 1?
- What does clause 10 say about indemnity for termset 3?
- What does clause 12 say about warranty waivers for termset 1?
- What does clause 12 say about remedies for CTM-P-ST-006?

Intent-focused queries
- What is the intent of clause 10 for termset 3?
- Why does clause 10 exist for termset 3?
- What is this clause for: clause 10 termset 3?
- What is the purpose of clause 12 for termset 1?

Common exceptions / pushback queries
- What common exceptions come up for clause 10 termset 3?
- What supplier pushback is common on clause 10 for termset 3?
- What common edits come up for clause 12 termset 1?
- What do suppliers object to in clause 12 for termset 6?

Suggested response queries
- What are the suggested responses for clause 10 termset 3?
- How should we respond to pushback on clause 10 termset 3?
- What should we say about clause 12 termset 1 warranty period?
- Give me the recommended response for clause 10 termset 4.

Alias / typo-friendly queries
- What is the intent of clause 10 for T&C 3?
- What common exceptions come up for clause 10 termet 3?
- Suggested responses for clause 12 tc 001
- Clause 10 termset 3 indemnity

No-answer / edge-case tests
- What does clause 10 say about launch insurance for termset 3?
- What is the intent of clause 3 for termset 2?
- What suggested responses are there for clause 12 termset 2 on spacecraft launch warranty?

Longer Multi-Turn Conversation Examples

Conversation 1: Missing termset, then retrieval
User: What does clause 12 say about warranty period?
Assistant: I have Clause 12 and your question. Which termset number should I search under?
User: termset 1
Assistant: Searches clause 12, termset 001, and answers on warranty period.

Conversation 2: Section-aware clause review
User: What is the intent of clause 10 for termset 3?
Assistant: Answers from the Intent section.
User: What common exceptions come up?
Assistant: Keeps clause 10 and termset 003, then routes to Common Exceptions / Suggested Responses.
User: How should we respond to that pushback?
Assistant: Keeps clause 10 and termset 003, then routes to Suggested Responses.
User: Can you explain that more?
Assistant: Keeps the current section focus and explains further.

Conversation 3: Update identifiers, keep the active question
User: What are the suggested responses for clause 10 for termset 3?
Assistant: Answers from Suggested Responses.
User: termset 4
Assistant: Re-runs the same suggested-responses question for clause 10, termset 004.
User: Clause 12
Assistant: Re-runs the same question for clause 12, termset 004.

Conversation 4: Switch from section-specific to general retrieval
User: What is the intent of clause 12 for termset 1?
Assistant: Answers from the Intent section.
User: What common exceptions come up?
Assistant: Switches to the Common Exceptions / Suggested Responses section.
User: What does the clause say about warranty period overall?
Assistant: Clears the old section focus and performs a general clause search.
User: Can you explain that more?
Assistant: Follows up on the current general answer.

Conversation 5: Negotiation-style workflow
User: What common exceptions come up for clause 10 termset 3?
Assistant: Returns the common supplier pushback points.
User: Which one is most risky?
Assistant: Answers from the retrieved evidence.
User: What should we say if the supplier deletes the duty to defend?
Assistant: Routes to Suggested Responses and answers from the matching response section.
User: Give me the short version.
Assistant: Summarizes the same response more concisely.

Conversation 6: Follow-up with same clause and termset
User: What does clause 10 say about indemnity for termset 3?
Assistant: Gives a grounded answer.
User: Clause 12
Assistant: Keeps the active query and termset, but changes the clause to 12.
User: termset 1
Assistant: Keeps the active query and clause, but changes the termset to 001.
User: Can you explain that more?
Assistant: Explains the current active result more clearly.
